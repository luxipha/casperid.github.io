# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 17, 2026

CasperID ("CasperID", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the CasperID platform, including our website, browser extensions, APIs, developer tools, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By accessing or using CasperID, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

* Create or manage a CasperID account
* Verify your identity (KYC/KYB), where required
* Use decentralized identity (DID) features
* Contact us for support or inquiries
* Integrate or develop applications using CasperID APIs

This information may include:

* Name, email address, and contact details
* Government-issued identification and verification data (where applicable)
* Organization or developer details
* Public blockchain addresses or decentralized identifiers (DIDs)

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Services, we may automatically collect:

* Device and browser information
* IP address and approximate location
* Usage data, logs, and interaction metadata
* Authentication events (e.g., passkey usage, login timestamps)

### 1.3 Blockchain and Decentralized Data

CasperID integrates with public blockchains and decentralized networks. Information recorded on a blockchain (such as wallet addresses, DIDs, or transaction hashes) is public, immutable, and not controlled by CasperID.

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

* **With Service Providers:** Trusted third parties that assist in operating the Services (e.g., identity verification providers, infrastructure partners).
* **With Developers or Applications:** Only with your explicit consent, when you authorize a third-party application to access specific identity attributes.
* **For Legal Compliance:** If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
* **Business Transfers:** In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

## 4. User Control and Data Minimization

CasperID is designed with privacy-by-design principles:

* You control which identity attributes are shared and with whom

* Wherever possible, sensitive data is stored off-chain and encrypted
* We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the Services
